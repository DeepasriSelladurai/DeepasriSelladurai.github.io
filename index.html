Volunteering

Indian Info Tech

Internship - 1 month



I completed an enriching internship at Indian Info Tech, where I delved into the world of the Internet of Things (IoT). Over six sessions, I gained foundational knowledge about IoT, exploring the blend of software and hardware that drives these embedded systems.

We traced the evolution of the internet from 1G to 5G, revisited the OSI model, and understood network uplink and downlink processes. The discussions on various IoT applications were particularly intriguing, sparking debates on whether IoT is a boon or a cause. I also learned about key IoT components, including connectivity, sensors, data processing, and user interfaces.

Hands-on sessions with microprocessors and microcontrollers like ATMEL, PIC, ARM, and Arduino (ESP-8266) provided practical experience.The final two sessions involved a blinking LED demo and a practical project, cementing my understanding and skills. This internship was a significant step in my journey toward mastering IoT.
